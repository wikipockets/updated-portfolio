About Me

Stephen Johnson was born in Charlotte NC. Stephen attened Appalachian State University starting in 2013, majoring in Computer Science and becoming a member of Pi Kappa Phi fraternity. Having a passion for music, Stephen became a member of the ASU Low Brass Ensemble, playing Tuba. Stephen returned to Charlotte to work as an IT tech for a company called Aloha Pools.

Stephen maintains his passion for music and art in his free time, playing Euphonium and harmonica. Currently working in the corporate Audio Video industry, Stephen has found a new hobby in lighting and video work. A fan of science fiction, Stephen also creates laser shows to his favorite tunes.
